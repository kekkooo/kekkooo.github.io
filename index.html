About

Ph.D. student

I'm a Ph. D. student at the Computer Graphics and Computational Geometry research group of the University of Cagliari. During summer 2014 I visited Danmarks Tekniske Universitet where I worked with J. Andreas Bærentzen My research is focused on building structured meshes such as Quad Meshes and Polar Annular Meshes (PAM's), both starting from an unstructured mesh or building them in a procedural fashion. I am particularly interested in exploring how structured meshes can be build and manipulated in combination with a shape descriptor such as curve-skeletons.
